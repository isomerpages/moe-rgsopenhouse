<h1>Curriculum Goals</h1>
<p>By the end of the course, you will be able to:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Apply mathematical concepts, principles and logic to solve problems</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Think independently, creatively and critically in the formulation of original
and elegant solutions</p>
</li>
</ul>
